Frequently Asked Questions

How long does the battery last?

The RaizPro X500 provides up to 40 hours of playback with noise cancellation enabled, and up to 60 hours with noise cancellation turned off.

Are these headphones waterproof?

The RaizPro X500 has an IPX4 rating, making them splash and sweat resistant, but not fully waterproof.

How do I connect to multiple devices?

The RaizPro X500 supports multipoint connection, allowing you to connect to two devices simultaneously. Simply pair with both devices and switch between them as needed.

What's included in the box?

Each purchase includes the RaizPro X500 headphones, a premium carrying case, USB-C charging cable, 3.5mm audio cable, and user manual.
